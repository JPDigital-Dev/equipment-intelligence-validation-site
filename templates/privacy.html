Privacy Policy

This validation site collects business contact information to support early-access conversations, demo requests, and pilot-program outreach.

What We Collect

We collect the information you submit through the early-access form, including your name, company name, email address, phone number, industry, company size, interest type, and operational challenge details.

Why We Collect It

We use this information to assess product demand, identify suitable pilot and demo candidates, and communicate about early product development opportunities.

How We Store It

Submitted data is currently stored in a local SQLite database as part of this validation-stage website. This setup is acceptable for early validation but should be upgraded for production-scale security and operations.

How We Use It

We use your submission only for product research, follow-up communication, and qualification for early-access discussions, pilots, and demos.
